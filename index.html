Who We Are

Founded by a seasoned bartender turned entrepreneur, Another Dirty Glass is the culmination of ten years of passion, expertise, and a relentless commitment to the art of mixology. With a background in tending bars at some of the most vibrant venues, Alondra brings a wealth of knowledge and a unique perspective to the world of mobile bar services.
Having honed skills in the bustling atmosphere of bars and clubs, Alondra understands the importance of precision, creativity, and exceptional customer service. This foundation is at the heart of Another Dirty Glass.
Join us on this exciting journey from behind the bar to the forefront of mobile mixology. Whether it's an intimate private party, a dazzling wedding, or a corporate affair, our mobile bar service is tailored to elevate your event to new heights. Discover the difference that experience, passion, and a personal touch can make. Cheers to more dirty glasses!
